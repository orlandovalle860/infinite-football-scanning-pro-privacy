Privacy Policy for Infinite Football Scanning Pro

Effective Date: December 2024

Introduction

Infinite Football Scanning Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our football training application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Infinite Football Scanning Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • User profiles (names, training preferences)
  • Training session history and statistics
  • Custom training configurations
  • App settings and preferences

This information is stored securely on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your data for any purposes. All app functionality operates using locally stored data only.

Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any user information to third parties. All data remains on your device and under your control.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No external servers are used for data storage
  • Your information is protected by your device's security measures

Children's Privacy

Infinite Football Scanning Pro is designed for football players of all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of the app.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. We do not use cookies, tracking technologies, or external data collection tools.

App Permissions

Infinite Football Scanning Pro requests the following permissions:
None currently required - The app operates without requiring access to camera, microphone, location, or other sensitive permissions.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists as long as the app is installed
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can manually delete profiles and training history within the app

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, traditional data subject rights (access, correction, deletion) do not apply. However, you have full control over your local data:

  • Create, edit, or delete user profiles
  • Clear training history
  • Reset app settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data
